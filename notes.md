If I walked into ResultsPRO, ExamsPRO, ClassroomPRO, TutorsPRO, and ScholarsNG today, I would want each to feel like a **specialist tool**, while SchoolHub feels like the **operating system**.

Think:

* SchoolHub = macOS
* ResultsPRO = Excel
* ExamsPRO = Google Forms + CBT Engine + International Exams Past Pappers
* ClassroomPRO = Google Classroom
* ScholarsNG = Duolingo + Coursera + GitHub for Kids
* TutorsPRO = Upwork + Calendly for Tutors

---

# The Most Important Rule

When someone is inside SchoolHub:

```text
I am running my school.
```

When someone is inside ResultsPRO:

```text
I am analyzing academics.
```

When someone is inside ExamsPRO:

```text
I am preparing for external exams.
```

When someone is inside ClassroomPRO:

```text
I am teaching/learnig.
```

When someone is inside TutorsPRO:

```text
I am finding learning support.
```

When someone is inside ScholarsNG:

```text
I am building future skills.
```

That's how you avoid the ecosystem becoming a confusing collection of menus.
